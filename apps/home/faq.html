What is Dogecoin?

Dogecoin is a decentralized, peer-to-peer digital currency that enables you to easily send money online. Think of it as "the internet currency."

What's up with the dog?

The dog's name is Kabosu and belongs to japanese kindergarten teacher Atsuko Sato. Atsuko Sato posted several photos of the Shiba Inu on her personal blog. Included in the photos was a shot of Kabosu casting a sideways glare at the camera. The gave way to the DOGE meme and subsequently become a sort of mascot for the Dogecoin currency
